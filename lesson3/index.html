Weather or Not

Weather Site Project Planning Document

Site name:

Weather or Not

Site purpose:

This website is for sharing the current weather for Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. This will help people determine what activites to pursue and enable them to choose the appropriate attire for their activities. The home page will include the current weather story with an image and information sections for each of the three towns. The town weather pages contain weather condition and other town information or relevant content. Images are used to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages. Responsive design will allow for desktop, tablet, and smartphone use.

Domain Name:

The domain name weatherornot.news will cost $30/year in addition to hosting services.
