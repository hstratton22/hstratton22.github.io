Weather or Not

Target Audience

Persona 1

Name: Vienne
Age: 5
Occupation: kindergartener
Family Status: youngest of 4 children
Goals: Needs to know what to wear to school each day
Tech skill: Minimal technological skill but literate
She needs something easily accessible that delivers current temperature immediately. Navigation and excess news are not important. Anything that will distract will make her late for school.
Scenarios: Should I wear rainboots and take my umbrella? Will I need a warm jacket or light jacket? Will it be nice weather to play at the park after school?


Persona 2

Name: Michael
Age: 43
Occupation: computer engineer/early morning seminary teacher
Family status: husband/father
Goals: Needs to know clothing plan but also potential traffic related to weather for commuting. Needs to plan physical activity inside or outside, depending on weather.
Tech skills: Expert technological skill level. Uses phone and Apple Watch for checking weather.
Needs accurate information when requested, but may be interested in additional news from time to time to plan future activities.
Scenarios: Do I need a coat or jacket this morning? Can I bike ride this afternoon? Do I need to leave work early to avoid traffic from bad weather conditions? Will school be cancelled due to weather? Will hiking trails be good condition this weekend? Can I fertilize the grass today?
