Excitement In the Air

by H. Stratton

Southern Idaho is not well know for its gorgeous landscapes. The barren hills roll endlessly. Patches of cattle graze contentedly. It is not uncommon to get lost in the monotony of the scenery. On occasion, however, heavenly intervention provides a magnificent view. Last Thursday, a storm danced across the state, and in the meantime, adventure-seeking photographers went to work. This magnificent display captures an intense moment of unleashed power was bravely captured by Max LaRochelle. As a safety reminder, when there is a storm, seek shelter immediately. Avoid tall structures, such as trees because they attract the reaching fingers of lightening. Remember, safety first!
